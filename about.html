About Me

I'm kind of a big Dill

Hello, my name is Dillon Tice but you can call me Dill. I'm currently a junior at Montana State University pursuing my bachelors degree in Computer Science.

Originally from Spokane, WA, I came to Montana State University in Bozeman mainly because I wanted to be in a place that offers both a college-town experience, as well as access to a vast natural playground known as the woods. Some of my hobbies include fly-fishing, skiing/snowboarding, and the production of music.

I'm actively seeking employment and internship opportunities to further my learning as a computer scientist. Feel free to contact me about either!
